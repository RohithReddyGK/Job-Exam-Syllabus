About Us

Our Job Exam Syllabus platform is designed to provide aspirants with structured and up-to-date syllabi for various competitive exams. This project aims to help candidates prepare effectively by offering a well-organized collection of exam syllabus for SSC, UPSC, Banking, and State PSCs in a single place.


Our platform covers:


  • Comprehensive Exam Syllabus

  • Structured Categorization

  • User-Friendly Navigation


Exams we cover:


SSC (Staff Selection Commission)

We offer detailed syllabus for SSC exams such as SSC CGL, SSC CHSL helping candidates prepare for government jobs.

UPSC (Union Public Service Commisision)

Covering the entire UPSC Civil Services syllabus, including Prelims and Mains for IAS, IPS, IFS, and other administrative services.

Banking Exams

Get the latest syllabus for banking exam like IBPS PO, finance-related government job.

State PSC (Public Service Commission)

Each state has its own PSC exam (KPSC, MPSC, TNPSC, etc.), and we provide the syllabus for Karnataka state administrative services.
